# Lab 1: Create and Explore a Free Tier AWS Account
## Objective: Create an AWS account, navigate the console, and identify free tier services.

## Prerequisites: None (AWS free tier available)

## Steps:

1. Go to https://aws.amazon.com/free/ and start the signup process.
2. Provide email, password, and account name; complete identity verification (phone).
3. In the AWS Management Console, switch to the Singapore (Singapore) region (or your preferred region).
4. Open the Billing and Cost Management dashboard.
5. Explore the Free Tier section to see what services are included in the free tier.
6. Look for the Services menu and locate at least 3 services you might use (e.g., S3, IAM, EC2, Lambda).


## Expected Results:

Account created and you can sign in to the console.
You can see Free Tier utilization and limits.
You can locate IAM, S3, and EC2 in the Services menu.

## Assessment / Checklist:

- Created AWS account
- Accessed Billing & Free Tier page
- Identified core services: IAM, S3, EC2 (or equivalents)
